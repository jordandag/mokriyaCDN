FROM THE THE MOUNTAINS AND TRIBES OF SIDAMA TO YOUR CUP

Whats Makes Our Coffee Unique

Completely organic coffee from a complete self-sustained and environment friendly farm!

Everything and everyone in our farm is mutually interconnected. From the water that flows from the mountains and the sunlight that shines down on the highlands to the coffee we produce and the dedicated people who maintain the farm. The river from the mountain sustains the coffee trees and is used to wash the coffee beans during the harvest season. The peeld coffee husks and the dung from the livestock in our farm will go back to the soil and act as a fertlizer to the coffee trees and other plants that sustain and act as a food to our livestock and workers in the farm. Even the drying racks for the coffee beans are all handmade from the bamboo grove behind the farm. Beside cutting off unnecessary expences, this makes our coffee farm throughly self-sustained and organic
