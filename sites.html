Our Site
It is situated in the middle of the coffee growing rigion of Sidama, Ethiopia and is full of mutually interconnected life producing a unique organic coffee


  • OUR SITES SECTION: IN OUR SITE WE HAVE A COFFE FARM AND CLEANING SITE… THE PROCESS IS LIKE BELOW.
  • After harvesting it from the tree the same day we peal the coffe beens using our machine and insert it to a water container for 3 days(72 hours) this makes the smallest peals come off the beans. Afte 3 days in a container the coffee will be throughly washed and taken to the raising banboo beds to be dried by the sun. depending on the sun the coffee will be dried from 15 days to 20 days. Finaly the dried coffee will be packed and delivered to the Exporters.
  • Time of harvest of coffeeSeptember – January : Season of harvest and delivery to the Exporters. November and December is the season we harvest most and during that time we have over 200 workers harvesting and processing the coffee dedicatedly
  • Amount of coffee is from 20 Isuzus to 50 Isuzus (Depends on capital: Right now our capital is 20 Isuzus). And we produce 1st grade Sidama Coffee.
  • The farm we have and its growing and washing and self-sustaining organic circle of coffee life environment.
  • Local SuppliersIntroduction to the local suppliers, how many they are and how we are helping eachother like by providing coffee byproducts as a fertelizer for their land. And how we intend to change their life quility.
